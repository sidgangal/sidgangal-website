# Instagram Post Template

## Format Guidelines
- Caption: 150-200 words, storytelling angle
- First line is the hook (shows in feed)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include call-to-action (link in bio)
- 20-30 hashtags in separate comment or at end
- Suggest carousel slide ideas for visual content

## Template Structure

```
[HOOK - First line that shows in feed preview]

[STORY - Personal angle or narrative hook in 2-3 short paragraphs]

[KEY INSIGHT - The main takeaway]

[CTA - Direct to bio link]

.
.
.

[HASHTAGS - 20-30 relevant tags]
```

## Example

```
I spent 47 hours on one stock. Here's why.

Sometimes you find a company with a story so unusual you have to dig deeper.

Nebius went from being worth $30 billion... to delisted... to sanctioned... to 500% gains in 15 months.

The founder spent 35 years building technology companies. He navigated his way out of Russia, rebuilt in the West, and landed $22B+ in contracts with Microsoft and Meta.

Most investors have never heard of them. That's usually when things get interesting.

Full breakdown in the link in my bio.

.
.
.

```

## Carousel Slide Ideas

For investment posts:
1. Hook slide with key stat
2. "What they do" simple explanation
3. Key numbers/metrics
4. Bull case (2-3 bullets)
5. Bear case (2-3 bullets)
6. CTA slide with link mention

For build posts:
1. Hook with founder lesson
2. The problem they solved
3. How they built it
4. Key insight
5. CTA slide

For thrive posts:
1. Hook with result/benefit
2. The challenge/problem
3. What I tried
4. What worked
5. CTA slide
