Fairview Software is Sid's current venture - an AI-Native Growth Stack focused on B2B Companies.

### The Big Idea

Fairview pairs a suite of interconnected AI tools to help grow revenue through a streamlined analytics + agentic workflow made for businesses.

The AI tools provide:

- Research knowledge
- A data lake with many companies' growth signals to learn how to improve our own signals
